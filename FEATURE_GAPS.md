# Feature Gap Analysis - Competitive Research Summary

This document identifies features that competitors offer which may represent opportunities for product enhancement.

---

## KitchenSync - Food Safety Platform

### Current Strengths
- Temperature logging (manual + IoT)
- Digital checklists with photo verification
- HACCP templates
- Batch tracking with lot codes
- Label printing (FIFO/date)
- Certification tracking
- Compliance dashboard

### Feature Gaps (Competitor Features We Lack)

| Feature | Competitor | Priority | Notes |
|---------|------------|----------|-------|
| **Employee Scheduling** | Jolt | Medium | Jolt includes drag-drop scheduling and time clock. Consider integration with scheduling apps vs building from scratch. |
| **AI-Powered HACCP Plan Generation** | FoodDocs | Low | FoodDocs can generate HACCP plans in 1 hour using AI. Nice-to-have but not core to daily operations. |
| **Incident Management Workflows** | Zenput | Medium | Zenput has formal incident reporting and tracking through resolution. Current corrective actions may suffice. |
| **QR Code Scanning for Verification** | Jolt | Medium | Jolt allows staff to scan QR codes to verify task completion. Adds accountability layer. |
| **Multi-Location Dashboard** | Jolt, Zenput | High | As customers grow, need better multi-location visibility. Currently positioning as single-location focused. |
| **Employee Time Clock** | Jolt | Low | Jolt includes photo + face detection clock-in. Better to integrate with dedicated time tracking apps. |

### Recommended Roadmap Priority
1. Multi-location management (for growth)
2. QR code verification (accountability)
3. Incident management workflows (compliance)

## EquipLog - Equipment Service Management

### Current Strengths
- Detailed equipment history and service logs
- Warranty tracking with expiration alerts
- Diagnostic logging (error codes, readings)
- Photo documentation
- Parts tracking with cost logging
- Smart scheduling with recurring reminders
- Route optimization (map view)
- Offline mode

### Feature Gaps (Competitor Features We Lack)

| Feature | Competitor | Priority | Notes |
|---------|------------|----------|-------|
| **QuickBooks Integration** | ServiceTitan, FieldEdge, Jobber | High | FieldEdge claims 20+ hrs/week saved. Critical for invoicing workflow. |
| **Customer Portal** | Jobber, ServiceTitan | Medium | Jobber's "Client Hub" lets customers self-serve. Good for customer experience. |
| **Pricebook with Supplier Pricing** | ServiceTitan | Low | ServiceTitan's Pricebook Pro has live supplier prices. Complex to implement. |
| **Marketing Automation** | ServiceTitan, Jobber | Low | Email campaigns, follow-ups. Not core to equipment tracking mission. |
| **Estimate/Quote Generation** | All competitors | High | All major competitors have quoting. Needed for complete job workflow. |
| **GPS Real-Time Tracking** | Housecall Pro, Jobber | Medium | Track technician locations in real-time. Housecall charges $20/vehicle/month. |
| **Payment Processing** | ServiceTitan, Housecall Pro | Medium | Collect payments in the field. Can integrate with Stripe/Square. |

### Recommended Roadmap Priority
1. QuickBooks integration (immediate value)
2. Estimate/quote generation (complete workflow)
3. Customer portal (customer experience)
4. Payment processing (field convenience)

## SafetyLens - Construction Safety Management

### Current Strengths
- OSHA-specific templates (scaffolding, trenching, fall protection)
- OSHA 300/300A/301 log generation
- Mobile inspections with photo documentation
- Full offline support
- Voice notes with transcription
- Dynamic form builder with conditional logic
- Safety analytics with heatmaps
- Super dailies (superintendent reports)
- Training and certification tracking
- Incident reporting and investigation

### Feature Gaps (Competitor Features We Lack)

| Feature | Competitor | Priority | Notes |
|---------|------------|----------|-------|
| **Multi-Language Support** | SiteDocs | High | SiteDocs offers English, French, Spanish. Important for diverse construction crews. |
| **External Contractor/Visitor Management** | SiteDocs | Medium | SiteDocs allows adding 400 external workers per account. Subcontractor tracking is valuable. |
| **Project Management Integrations** | iAuditor, Procore | Medium | iAuditor integrates with Procore, Teams, Gmail. Consider Procore integration for enterprise. |
| **Equipment Maintenance Scheduling** | Safesite | Low | Safesite tracks equipment maintenance. Overlaps with EquipLog positioning. |
| **AI/Predictive Analytics** | SiteDocs | Medium | SiteDocs has AI-powered analysis. Could enhance our analytics with predictions. |
| **SOC 2 Compliance Certification** | SiteDocs | High | SiteDocs is SOC 2 and GDPR compliant. Important for enterprise sales. |
| **API Access** | iAuditor, Procore | Medium | Needed for enterprise integrations and custom workflows. |

### Recommended Roadmap Priority
1. Multi-language support (Spanish priority for US construction)
2. SOC 2 compliance (enterprise sales enabler)
3. Subcontractor/visitor management
4. Procore integration (enterprise construction)
